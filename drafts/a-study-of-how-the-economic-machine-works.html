Introduction

Ray Dalio is a very successful investment banker (he is worth about $15 Billion). In Sep 2013, he published a youtube video explanation explaining how the economy works. For a subject that can get very technical with huge amounts of jargon, Dalio managed make it both simple and concise. Nevertheless, if you are approaching this topic with no prior knowledge of finance (or you don't work in the field in your everyday life), there are lots of terms and definitions that you have to understand.

I am going to attempt to analyze Dalio's video explanation in a way that empowers readers with no background knowledge in finance.
